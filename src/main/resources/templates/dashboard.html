UPI Offline Mesh Simulator

A high-fidelity simulation of decentralized, deferred-settlement offline UPI transaction routing. Packets are cryptographically signed/encrypted and hop peer-to-peer (P2P) across mesh networks until a bridge detects internet gateway connectivity.
